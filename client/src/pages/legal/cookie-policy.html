Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2024

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Enlighten ED ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. By continuing to browse our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners about how users interact with their site.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility.

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We use these cookies to help us understand how visitors interact with our website, which helps us improve our services. They collect information anonymously.

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your preferences and settings.

3.4 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, helping us improve its performance and user experience.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your login status and preferences
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Improve our website's performance and security
  • Provide relevant content and recommendations

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Please note that removing or blocking cookies may impact your user experience and some features of our website may not function properly.

Browser Settings

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

6. Third-Party Cookies

Some of our pages may contain content from third-party services (like video platforms or analytics tools). These services may use their own cookies. We do not control these cookies and recommend checking the privacy policies of these third parties.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
